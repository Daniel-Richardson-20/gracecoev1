Transport

GRACE College of Engineering has a fleet of several buses to facilitate the students and staff to reach the campus on time. The bus services cover a wide area covering the nearby cities, towns and villages. The buses are also utilized for industrial visits. As the College is located along the Tuticorin - Tiruchendur main road, it is well reachable by scores of public transport buses and many day scholar students use this service.

Digital library

Digital Library in Grace College of Engineering is a virtual repository of learning resources which is not just a repository with search/browse facilities but provides a host of services for the learner community. It computer systems for students’ reference. Membership is open to all the staff and the students of Grace college of Engineering. Students can access to e-journals and text journals covering Engineering & Tech. and Computer Science Journals. Abstract and Citation database in the field of Science, Technology, Social Science, Arts and Humanities, with so many titles from bunch of publishers. Library has xerox machine inside the digital library for the students’ purpose. Students can come at any time with the signature from the respective staff and HoD.

Library

Grace college of Engineering has an excellent library for the benefit of students, faculties and researchers. As one of the best-established libraries according to International Standards, the University Library has a large collection of books covering various branches of Engineering and Technology, Computer Science and Life Science books, Science & Humanities Including Arts, Management, Languages Text. Competitive Exam Books, etc., Theses & Dissertations, Projects reports, Back Volumes, Reference Books, and Phonetics and communication and its related fields. The library is more than a decade old. The library is well Protected with fire alarms and CCTV Security systems. The library is located in the Administrative Block, and is spread over the whole first floor of spacious area. The library building is well protected with fire alarms and CCTV security systems equipped with reading halls, with reference collection.

Communication Lab

The Department of English has a well-stocked library catering to the varied interests of students and faculty. The Language Laboratory is equipped with computers, LCD projector, CD players and necessary software which aid in the development of LSRW (Listening, speaking,reading,Writing) skills in English. The Language Laboratory also has an area outfitted with a lectern and sound system, earmarked for presentations and group discussions. With a view to motivating the slow learners and enhancing their communication skills a multi media laboratory has been set up with internet and online facilities. This also helps to train students who appear for TOEFL, IELTS, GRE and other language competitive examinations.

Canteen

Timing:9.30am-4.30pm

The canteen serves break fast and lunch for the students. Tea and coffee are available in the canteen. And it also has some stationery items for students’ purpose.

Internet facility

It currently has infrastructure backbone of 100 Mbps from Internet service providers. High capacity 100 Mbps Internet Connectivity is given in the campus through LAN connectivity and wifi as well. Each department has WIFI connection with swift speed for their department use. And additionally, students can use the internet connectivity through wifi in the library desktop computers for their project or seminar preparation purpose by the signature from the concern staff and HoD. Internet related issues will be handled by system admin.

Gymnasium In Boys Hostel

For students to stay fit and healthy, all hostels are equipped with gym and fitness centre facilities - treadmill, exercise bikes and more of the latest fitness equipment.The gym for men at Grace College of Engineering is also equipped with body building equipment. All gyms and fitness centers are open both in the morning and evening hours.

Sports

Grace College of Engineering’s sports and games are comprehensive programmes that incorporate many activities during an academic year. Activities available to students encompass indoor and outdoor sports such as Basketball, Badminton, Chess, Cricket, Carom, Table Tennis, Tennis, Volleyball, Yoga and many other worthwhile recreational choices. At Grace College of Engineering we encourage our students to become fully involved in the Sports Programme in order to take advantage of the opportunities being offered. The eclectic mix of activities gives Grace College of Engineering a vibrant atmosphere and gives the students the opportunity to become risk takers, make lasting friendships and develop skills and passions beyond the academic timetable. Students participate regularly in Inter Collegiate, Inter University and State Level Tournaments.
